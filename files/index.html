About Us

We are a team of experienced software developers and educators who are passionate about helping people learn the skills they need to succeed in the tech industry.

  • One-on-One mentorship
  • Global Accreditation
  • Guaranteed internship

Fueled by the same innovative spirit and expertise that made Melsoft a standout in the tech industry, our academy is on a mission to address youth unemployment in Africa by equipping students with essential tech skills, work experience, and job placements.

Our specialized bootcamp-style programs are designed to empower the next generation of software developers, focusing on practical, hands-on experience, and community engagement.
Through income-share agreements and job guarantees, we are committed to removing financial barriers and opening doors to fulfilling and lucrative tech careers for youth across the continent.
